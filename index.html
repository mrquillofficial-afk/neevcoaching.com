Common Questions

Parents Ask, We Answer

What is NEEV and what makes it unique? ▼
NEEV is a dedicated coaching and academic institution focused on preparing students for entrance exams of prestigious schools. Our unique blend of experienced faculty, structured curriculum, and personalized mentoring — combined with holistic character development — sets us apart from typical coaching centres.
What exams does NEEV prepare students for? ▼
We prepare students for Sainik School Entrance (AISSEE), Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test (JNVST), Rashtriya Military School (RMS) Entrance, Ramakrishna Mission Schools Entrance, CHS (Central Hindu School) Varanasi, and Netarhat Residential School.
Is hostel facility available? ▼
Yes. We provide safe, hygienic, and disciplined hostel facilities for outstation students with round-the-clock care. Separate dormitories are maintained for boys and girls, with 24×7 CCTV monitoring and trained wardens on duty at all times.
What if my child is not selected in the entrance exam? ▼
At NEEV, we believe every child is unique and capable. If a student is not selected, they still receive a strong academic foundation aligned with CBSE/State Board standards. We help identify their strengths and interests, provide remedial support, one-on-one mentoring, and guide them toward alternative school admissions and scholarships.
Will these schools benefit my child even if they don't want a military career? ▼
Absolutely. Military schools focus on discipline, fitness, leadership, structured routines, and academic excellence. These traits benefit every career path. Many alumni of these schools become doctors, engineers, civil servants, and business leaders — the military background is an advantage, not a constraint.
What academic support do students receive beyond coaching? ▼
Alongside entrance preparation, NEEV ensures a strong academic foundation with regular school syllabus support, frequent tests, and personality development sessions. We groom students holistically so they excel both in competitive exams and in regular school.
